About Me

Hello!

My name is Brett Morrison. I’m 23 and a student of computer science at Western Washington University in Bellingham, WA. I originally was studying at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, BC but transferred after my 3rd (ish) year.

I grew up in Hood River, OR and love lots of outdoor activities. Mostly mountain scrambling right now, but mountain biking, skiing, and windsurfing/foiling are all also favorites of mine.

I enjoy a lot of different things at different times, and this is a fun way for me to keep track of those ventures… The creation of this site was a venture of its own! There is room for this to grow as I grow, so don’t get too attached to anything on here. I look forward to updating this and adding fun new features as time goes on.

I hope you enjoy, learn something from, or are inspired by my creative expression, have a wonderful day!
